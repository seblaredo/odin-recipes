Pasta Bolognese

A delicious pasta that I love and eat all of the time! It is made up of your favorite type of pasta, as well as tomato sauce with ground beef. Absolutely delicious.

Ingredients

  • 500g Ground beef
  • Cooking oil
  • 300g pasta
  • One jar of bolognese sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Boil water on a big pot
  2. While it boils, start cooking your ground beef with plentiful oil in a separate pan. Add salt, garlic powder, and pepper to taste.
  3. Once brown, add jar of bolognesa sauce to the ground beef and mix
  4. Cook your pasta according to set time on package
  5. Serve together and enjoy!
