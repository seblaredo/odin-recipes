Steak Pasta

A creamy delicacy that I can barely ever afford to make but absolutely love top enjoy. Super filling!

Ingredients

  • 200-400g steak
  • Cooking oil
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 3 bellpeppers
  • 1 onion
  • 30g taco seasoning
  • 350g protein pasta
  • 250ml cooking cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3/4 cup salsa
  • 50g cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare your steak seasoning by combining 15g of taco seasoning and olive oil
  2. Rub seasoning on steak and cook in a pan with a bit of cooking oil until done
  3. Add butter into pan and cover steak with it as it melts
  4. Remove steak and cut into bite sized cubes
  5. Add bellpeppers, onion and garlic, stir fry for 5 minutes
  6. Add remaining taco seasoning and stir
  7. Add cooking cream and salsa to vegetables and stir until hot
  8. Add cheese to mix
  9. Combine with pasta and enjoy together!!
