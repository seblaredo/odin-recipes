Stuffed Peppers

The most delicious stuffed pepper recipe ever. I literally cant I love this recipe so much. Actually perfect for every ocassion ever.

Ingredients

  • 4-6 bell peppers
  • Cooking oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 5-6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 15 oz tomato sauce
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozarella

Steps

  1. Cut the top of the bellpeppers and remove the seeds and insides so it creates a hollow container
  2. Place in air fryer at 400C for 5 minutes
  3. In pan add minced onion and garlic, stir for a few minutes
  4. Add ground beef, cook until brown
  5. Add the tomato sauce and the spices and mix
  6. Add cooked rice and mix
  7. Aseemble bellpeppers by adding stuffing until the middle, then 15g of cheese, then stuff up to the top
  8. When ready to eat, add another 15g of cheese on top and cook in air fryer on 350C for 7 minutes
